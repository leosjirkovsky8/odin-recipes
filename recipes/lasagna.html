Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the delicious results are well worth it. This recipe is loved for its flexibility, allowing you to adjust ingredients to your taste. If you follow the classic version, you’ll need sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef, onion, garlic, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and a bit of sugar to balance the flavor. It's seasoned with parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper. You’ll also need lasagna noodles (store-bought or homemade), along with a rich blend of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese. An egg helps bind the ricotta. To make the lasagna, first prepare the meat sauce, cook the noodles, and mix the ricotta. Then layer everything in the following order: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, more meat sauce, and Parmesan — repeating until you’re out of ingredients. Bake the assembled lasagna at 375°F (190°C) for about 50 minutes, covering it with foil for the first half, then letting it brown uncovered. Finally, allow it to rest for 15 minutes before slicing to let the layers set properly.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking the Italian sausage, ground beef, chopped onion, and minced garlic in a large skillet. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and sugar to the meat mixture. Season with parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes.
  3. While the sauce simmers, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and a bit of parsley.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Assemble the lasagna: Start with a layer of meat sauce, then noodles, then ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella slices. Repeat layers. Finish with meat sauce and sprinkle Parmesan on top.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake uncovered for another 25 minutes.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before serving to help it set.
